Our Solution

Protects your workforce.

OUR MISSION

To continually advance safety by use of wearable technology and data modeling.

ItusOne seeks to make the world a safer place for those who build it, protect it, and work in it. Through the use of sophisticated wearable technology and advanced data analytics, ItusOne advances safety protocols and modernizes safety technology.

WEARABLES FOR SAFETY

Safety is created by common-sense protocols, and the proper equipment.

ItusOne's safety solution utilizes wearable technology to track workforce data and provides real-time text message notifications about the state of your crew members. This data will soon also be given in our dashboard, and analytics are provided to give you the data you need to make the surest safety protocols.

Data is Collected.

Your crew members wearing the device will have vital information collected. This information will be sent to our servers for analysis, and your onsite crew members will be notified via text message of important events in your workforce.

You are Notified.

Important crew member events are sent to the onsite management team. This information allows you to provide proactive safety measures that protect your workforce.

Data is Analyzed.

Once the data reaches our servers we perform propreitary analysis of trends. The models of this data are created for each of your safety needs.

Data is Monitored.

After the data reaches the server, it is also sent back to you in our dashboard or API products, which will soon be available. You have access to our dashboard specific to your organization, which will allow you to monitor your crew members in real time.
