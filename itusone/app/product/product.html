WEARABLES FOR SAFETY

Safety is created by common-sense protocols, and the proper equipment.

ItusOne's safety solution utilizes wearable technology to track workforce data and provides real-time insight into the state of your crew members. This data is given in our dashboard, and analytics are provide to give you the data you need to make the surest safety protocols.

Data is Collected.

Your crew members wearing the device will have vital information collected. This information will be sent to our servers for analysis.

Data is Analyzed.

Once the data reaches our servers we perform propreitary analysis of trends. The models of this data are created for each of your safety needs.

Data is Monitored.

After the data reaches the server, it is also sent back to you. You have access to our dashboard specific to your organization, which will allow you to monitor your crew members in real time.
